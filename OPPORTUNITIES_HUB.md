# Opportunities Hub

The Opportunities Hub is a beta research view built from completed prediction records. It does not change prediction scoring, provider integrations, or ranking logic.

## Purpose

The hub helps users filter the existing prediction set by investor profile, timeframe, price band, and ranking style.

## Controls

- Investor View: All Opportunities, Beginner Picks, Penny Speculative.
- Timeframe: 1-Day, 7-Day, 1-Month, 1-Year.
- Price Band: all bands, $5 and under, $5.01-$10, $10.01-$50, $50.01-$100, $100.01 and up.
- Ranking View: overall score, confidence, lower risk, momentum, most improved, new to list.
- Investment Amount Preview: $25, $50, $100, $250, $500, or custom.

## Important Guardrail

The investment access preview is a convenience calculation. It is not a portfolio recommendation and does not replace position sizing or risk management.
