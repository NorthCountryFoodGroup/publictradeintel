# Phase 2 Roadmap

Phase 2 is planning only; none of these items are implemented by Phase 1.

1. Add sourced historical market data for returns, moving averages, volume baselines, breakout, and reversal evidence.
2. Add sourced fundamental and earnings data.
3. Build sector return, breadth, and leadership modeling.
4. Build sourced macro and aggregate market-regime modeling.
5. Calibrate the unchanged prediction system against real outcomes before proposing scoring changes.
6. Expand outcome tracking with leakage-safe evaluation windows.
7. Add performance analytics by bucket, regime, timeframe, and evidence quality.
8. Calibrate confidence against observed hit rates and drawdowns.
9. Add portfolio-aware diversification and optimization after candidate quality is validated.
10. Establish a bounded canary deployment and observation-history process.
11. Define separately approved automatic-promotion policy only after sustained readiness and rollback validation.
