# AI Best Ideas

AI Best Ideas is the first section in the Stocks to Buy Center.

It is not simply the top 10 by unified score. The selection layer uses completed scan fields including:

- unified prediction score
- confidence score
- market data quality
- risk score
- conflicting signal count
- fallback usage
- missing critical fields
- score improvement
- risk/reward where available

## Diversification

The selection caps a sector at two ideas where qualified alternatives exist within a reasonable score range.

## Output

Each Best Idea includes rank, ticker, company, score, confidence, risk, reason, caution, data quality, reference price, provider/fallback status, and Trade Brief actions.
