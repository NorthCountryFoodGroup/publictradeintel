# Investment Access Preview

Investment Access Preview helps users understand whether a stock is accessible at small dollar amounts.

Supported amounts:

- $25
- $50
- $100
- $250
- $500
- $1,000
- custom

For each stock, the preview shows:

- whole shares
- whether fractional shares may be required
- estimated fractional shares
- remaining cash after whole shares
- percent of selected amount needed for one share

This is a convenience calculation only. It is not position-size advice.
