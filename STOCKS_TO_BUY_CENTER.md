# Stocks to Buy Center

Version 2.2 Sprint 2 adds the Stocks to Buy Center as the primary ranked-buy-ideas destination.

The center uses completed prediction scan records only. Changing filters does not rerun the scan and does not modify prediction scoring, adaptive weighting, provider integrations, or ranking engine inputs.

## Main Views

- AI Best Ideas: up to 10 cross-qualified ideas selected from completed scan records using score, confidence, data quality, risk, fallback usage, missing fields, and sector diversification.
- Timeframe lists: 1 Day, 7 Days, 1 Month, and 1 Year.
- Price categories: Overall, $5 and under, $5.01-$10, $10.01-$25, $25.01-$100, and over $100.
- Investor views: All Opportunities, Beginner Picks, and Penny & Speculative.
- Ranking views: Top 25, Highest Confidence, Lowest Risk, Highest Momentum, Most Improved, and New Entrants.

## Card Content

Each card displays ticker, company, reference price, price timestamp, category, ranks, score, recommendation, confidence, conviction, risk, direction, timeframe, reasons, caution, data quality, freshness, provider/fallback status, actions, and Investment Access Preview.

The center is a research interface, not position-size advice.
