# Ranking Explanations

Trade Brief 2.0 explains why a stock ranked without changing the ranking model.

Displayed fields can include:

- Overall rank.
- Timeframe rank.
- Price-band rank.
- Beginner Picks rank.
- Penny & Speculative rank.
- Previous rank.
- Rank change.
- Score change.
- Time in Top 25.
- New entrant status.
- Primary ranking reasons.
- Largest negative contribution.

Primary ranking reasons come from existing `signalContribution`, `signalContributions`, `modelScores`, or stored strongest signals. Missing contribution details are labeled as unavailable instead of invented.
