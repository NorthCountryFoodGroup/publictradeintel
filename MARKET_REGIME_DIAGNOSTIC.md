# Market Regime Diagnostic

The Market Regime Diagnostic is informational only in Version 2.2 Sprint 1.

It may classify the environment as:

- Trending Bullish.
- Trending Bearish.
- Sideways / Range-Bound.
- Mixed / Transitioning.
- Unavailable.

Inputs can include:

- Broad-market proxy trend.
- Prediction-universe bias.
- Breadth inside the analyzed prediction universe.
- Volatility proxy where available.
- Sector participation.
- Trend dispersion.

This sprint does not dynamically change prediction weights based on regime. Adaptive weighting belongs in a later sprint after validation.
