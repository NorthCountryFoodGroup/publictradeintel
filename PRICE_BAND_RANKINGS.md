# Price Band Rankings

Price band rankings are calculated from completed prediction records in the Opportunities Hub.

## Bands

- $5 and under.
- $5.01-$10.
- $10.01-$50.
- $50.01-$100.
- $100.01 and up.
- Price unavailable.

## Ranking Metadata

Each Opportunities Hub card can show:

- Overall rank.
- Price-band rank.
- Investor-view rank when an investor filter is active.
- Freshness status.

Trade Briefs also show available rank metadata so the user can understand why a ticker appears in a filtered opportunity view.
