# Stocks to Buy Qualification

The Stocks to Buy Center uses completed prediction records and applies qualification rules before display.

## Required

- valid prediction score for the selected timeframe
- valid positive price
- reference price timestamp
- non-mixed, known direction where required
- sufficient data quality
- acceptable confidence tier
- supported exchange/security type
- no missing critical fields
- no critical risk warning

## Safeguards

The center does not weaken qualification standards to fill 25 results.

Beginner Picks apply additional clarity, liquidity, quality, and risk screens.

Penny & Speculative keeps high-risk warnings visible and should be treated as research only.

Provider fallbacks and stale data are shown on the card instead of hidden.
